Features

Grades are always a percentage.

Ever had a course using standard grades? These grades are representive of objective scoring, showcasing competion of each objective. However these standards do not show your score; if your passing or failing. Making it a guessing game when it comes to grades. This app allows you to see your courses grades as a percentage always.

Instantly get your semester GPA.

Need to know what your GPA will be? Use the GPA calculator feature of Gradpass! Easily calculate your GPA at the semester with your current grades.

Includes:

Choose between a half or full semester course.
Choose if a course is an AP or regular class.
After setting up what each class is, it will constantly update your GPA based on your current grades.
Knowing your GPA is important! Make sure it's high up there easily and quickly using this feature.

Have complex details of each specific course.

Never miss what your objective scores are again! All objective scores for a course are always shown on the courses page. The courses page also shows grades for every bit of work you have completed.

Hide any course that you do not need anymore.

Have an expired course? Or a course that you are not in anymore yet still exists in your courses page? Easily remove them with this feature. Simply click on a course name to hide it completely from your account. This also accounts for GPA, Average Objectives, and Average Score grades.
